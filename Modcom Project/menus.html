Reserve Wine List

Reserve wine is wine of a higher quality than usual, or a wine that has been aged before being sold, or both. Traditionally, winemakers would reserve some of their best wine rather than sell it immediately, coining the term. The Reserve is a unique wine created from selections by Albert Allaham and Alessandro during their journey to Israel at the beginning of 2020. After tasting multiple premium barrels from a single vineyard, The Reserve blend was born. The result is a balanced medium to full body blend of Cabernet Sauvignon & Merlot.
