ABOUT US

Buen Provecho started as a catering company in 2007 introducing Puerto Rican food to metro Atlanta. During this time we quickly noticed Georgia’s misconception of Latin food; they’re all the same. We were determined to prove otherwise. As Buen Provecho gained popularity, in 2011 we launched the first Puerto Rican cuisine food truck in GA, educating locals of the diversity in Latin cuisine. While bringing exposure of our “comida criolla”, we were dedicated to offer only the finest in quality food and cater all ethnicities. In 2015, we brought you the first Buen Provecho Restaurant in Atlanta and we couldn't have been more thrilled to provide a great new place to enjoy in the area. Opening our first restaurant has been a labor of love and we've developed our menu with the hope that each and every dish becomes someone's absolute favorite. Now in 2019, we have brought the Buen Provecho Restaurant down to Cape Coral, FL. Come check us out!
